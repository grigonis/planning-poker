Recommendation

B1 (inline, in document flow) is simpler and avoids all z-index/emoji conflicts — emojis animate above the table, stats sit calmly below. On mobile, the stats section is just a scroll away. A drawer adds native-feel on mobile but introduces overlay complexity and potential emoji conflicts that need careful z-index management.

Hybrid idea: Inline panel that collapses to a single "View Stats ▲" toggle bar by default — tap to expand. No drawer, no z-index issues, works perfectly on mobile.
